Preface

In summer of 2018, I got the chance to do a bit of travelling - travelled Europe in May, and Southeast Asia from mid-June to late July. It was sweet, so I’m writing it down.

While my trip started in Hong Kong and ended in Singapore, I’m going to start with Vietnam as a whole. Vietnam was overwhelmingly my favourite part of the journey. You’ll often hear the maxim of ‘vacation vs travel’, with the latter being a busier and more active way of moving through a trip. If this is you, Vietnam has much to offer.

Hoping this stirs my nostalgia and helps some friends unto the breach. Bess, Tim, El, this is for you dingos too.
